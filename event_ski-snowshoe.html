February 4th

3rd Annual Riverside Trails Candlelit Ski and Snowshoe

A path lit by Luminaries

Join us for the third annual Candlelit Ski and Snowshoe event at the Riverside Trails. This is a family friendly night event featuring two loops through the woods lit by over 500 luminaries. There will also be a bonfire, warm beverages, and food for participants. It's a great time!

This is a non-timed, go-at-your-own-pace, HAVE FUN event


Location: Riverside Trails in Niagara

Date: Saturday, February 4th

Start Times: 5:15pm Registration | 5:45pm Skiers | 6:15pm Snowshoers & Skiers

What to Bring: A headlamp, your skis or snowshoes and friends.

Register Early: Individual ($10) or as a Family of three or more ($30).

Register Event Day: Individual ($15) or as a Family of three or more ($30).

Ready to go? Register at Active.com or fill out a registration form here and either mail it in or drop it off at participating Niagara businesses.

Proceeds: Are used for scholarship donations and other community needs.

Event Sponsors: Thank you to our sponsors for this event!
