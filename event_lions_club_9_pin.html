February 17th-18th

Niagara Lions Bowling Tournament

Niagara Lions Bowling Tournament!

The Niagara Lions will host their Ninth Annual Niagara Lions-9 Pin Bowling Tournament on Friday-Saturday, Feb. 16-17, at Niagara’s Uptown Lanes. Gordon Behnke is again serving as director for the popular event he will be ably assisted by Brad Butler who will again serve as emcee. Prizes will be awarded and proceeds from the tournament will be used for Lions projects to benefit the community.

While the tournament will be competitive, the tournament is primarily a “fun event” which is open to bowlers and non-bowlers alike. Handicaps will be used and one will be assigned to those without them. The format will feature both a singles competition as well as team competition.

The singles division will be held on Friday at 6:30 and 9:00pm and at noon on Saturday.

The team event is scheduled for Saturday with competitions taking place at 3:00pm and at 6:00pm.

Cost to enter is $25.00 per bowler and prize money will be awarded to the top individuals and teams. Food and beverages will be available, 50-50 raffles will be held, and many other prizes will be given out throughout the tournament.

Registration forms are available at Uptown Lanes (715-251-1700) . Entry checks should be made out to the Niagara Lions Club.

Tournament chairman, Gordon Behnke, may be reached at 906-221-7583 or by email at nt1@borderlandnet.net
