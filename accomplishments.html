Ways we are helping the community

Helping our future professionals succeed.

June 2017

The Niagara Area Business Association (NABA) recently awarded Mackenzie McCarthy with a $500.00 scholarship to help in her efforts to pursue a degree in psychology with an emphasis on substance abuse at the University of Oshkosh.

Read more here!

Another Successful Winter Event!

February 2017

The event featured two lighted loops, over 300 hundred luminaries, a bonfire, hot dogs, hot drinks, even a warming shack with refreshments in the woods. Almost $2,000 were raised to support maintenance of the Niagara Riverside Trails as well as supporting the Niagara School System and local community. Thank you to all the volunteers and to the many participants for making this a great event!

Read more here!

Collecting Food for those in need.

October 2016

The annual Fall Fill the bus where Niagara Area Businesses and the School collected food for for Karens food pantry. Karen's food panty helps to supply food locally to those in need.

Riverside Trail Signs

August 2016

We have a beautiful set of hiking, biking, and ski trails known as the Riverside Trails in Niagara. Some of the proceeds from the 2016 Ski/Snowshoe event went to signs to raise awareness of this community gem.

Decorating the Gazebo

May 2016

Thanks to NABA members Juli and husband Jim for decorating the Gazebo in preparation for the summer. It looks fantastic!

Niagara School Scholarship

April 2016

The Niagara Area Business Assocation donated $500 for a scholarship for seniors at the Niagara High School. Some of the proceeds from the 2016 Ski/Snowshoe event were set aside for this cause.
